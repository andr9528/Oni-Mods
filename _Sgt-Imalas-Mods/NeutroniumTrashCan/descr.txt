[h1]Trash Cans[/h1]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/GbFtLZ2.png[/img]

This mod adds trash cans that allow you to dispose of unwanted materials.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Adds 4 Trash Can buildings to the game that delete any items put into them
[*] Neutronium Trash Can will delete all "special"-type materials, mainly those neutronium chunks from rocket shaving
[*] Solid Trash Can will delete all solids shipped into it via conveyor rail
[*] Liquid Trash Can will delete all liquids pumped into it via pipe
[*] Gas Trash Can will delete all gasses pumped into it via pipe
[*] Trash Cans are 1x1 and have to be placed on the ground/wall ceiling (rotatable)
[*] Trash Cans are unlocked by the Smart Storage research, found under "Utility" and cost 100 raw metal each.
[/list]
