[h1]Scan & Relay Satellites(DLC)[/h1]

This mod adds the opportunity to deploy satellites on the starmap that function as logic relays and scan the surrounding space tiles.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] New Rocket Module: Satellite Carrier Module - Deploy and retrieve satellites with this module
[*] New crafting item: Satellite Parts - Construct these at the workbench. One satellite takes 20 satellite parts to make.
[*] New Mechanic: Satellites - When deployed on the space map, these satellites provide 2 functions:[list]
    [*] Scanner: Will slowly reveal the space around it, similar to a telescope
    [*] Logic Relay: Will relay logic signals to increase the range of logic broadcasters. See the images for visual explanation.
    [/list]
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Mod Config[/h1]
[list]
[*] Satellite Scan Range: Radius of the max. satellite scan distance. Higher value == more of the space map gets revealed.
[*] Satellite Scan Speed: Time the satellites scanner need to reveal one space tile (value in cycles). 
[*] Satellite Logic Repeater Range: The range the satellites connect logic broadcasts.
[/list]
