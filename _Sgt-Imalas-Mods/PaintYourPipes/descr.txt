[h1]Paint your Pipes & Wires[/h1]

This mod allows you to color pipes, rails, wires and logic components. This coloring can also be toggled to show in overlays.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.1.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Paint Gas pipes, liquid pipes, shipping rails, power wires, logic wires, logic components
[*] Overlay Color Toggle: switch between the regular overlay mode and the painted version (also comes with hotkey, by default shift + C, this can be adjusted in the normal hotkey settings)
[*] Color Sharing: you can copy the color of items of the same type via a "copy settings" button
[*] Color whole network: share the selected color with all connected pipes/wires in the network (follows vanilla highlighting rules for network definition).
[*] "Overlay Only" mode (activatable in the config) - only overlays display selectable colors, material color mods take effect
[/list]

[h1]Mod Integrations[/h1]
[list][*] Material Color Mods: your selected paint colors will take priority over the material color
[*] Reverse Bridges: Compatibility Patch in place
[*] Gigawatt Wire: wires and bridges of this mod are made paintable 
[*] Insulated Wire Briges: bridges of this mod are made paintable 
[*] High Pressure Applications: pipes of this mod are made paintable 
[/list]
