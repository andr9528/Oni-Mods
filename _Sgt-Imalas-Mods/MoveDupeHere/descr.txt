[h1]"Move Here" Command Tile[/h1]

This mod adds a tile that allows to call duplicants to its position via logic signal

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]New Tile: "Duplicant Caller Tile"[/h1]
[list]
	[*]made from 25kg of refined metal and 75kg of raw minerals
	[*]requires 10W of power.
	[*]requires logic cable
	[*]requires duplicant assigned
	[*]send a green logic signal to continuously call the assigned duplicant to the tiles location (only works if the dupe can reach the tile)
	[*]cancel ongoing movement calls via red logic signal.
	[*]move commands override dupes need, life insurance not included
	[*]Colour indicator: Grey = no power/no dupe assignment, Red = dupe assigned but no commands given, Green = dupe is being called
[/list]
