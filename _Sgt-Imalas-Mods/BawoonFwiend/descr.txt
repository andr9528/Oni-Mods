[h1]Balloon Dispenser[/h1]

This mod adds a ballon dispenser rec building that hands out balloons to dupes in their downtime

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.1.1[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] New Building: Balloon Dispenser, found under Furniture/Recreation.
[*] Requires 250KG of refined metal.
[*] Unlocked with the research "Home Luxuries".
[*] Gives out a balloon at the cost of 5kg of ballon gas; either hydrogen or helium (found in other mods, f.e. Baator).
[*] Gives a less strong ballon buddy buff (+3 by default, can be changed in mod config).
[*] Has Selection screen that allows to individually enable/disable unlocked ballon skins and switch between random and queue based skin selection
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Integration[/h1]
This mod has integration with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2293939893] Varicoloured Balloons[/url].
When Varicoloured Balloons is installed, it's skins become available for selection in the balloon dispenser.

This mod is fully translatable! 
You can find the translation template under ~\Documents\Klei\OxygenNotIncluded\mods\strings_templates
The template for this mod is called "bawoonfwiend_template.pot".
If you want to have a specific translation added, you can create a translation file with that template and send it to me via GitHub.
