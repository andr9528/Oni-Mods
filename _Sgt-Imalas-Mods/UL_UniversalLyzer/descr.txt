[h1]Universal Electrolyzer[/h1]

This mod allows the electrolyzer to electrolyze all types of water. the output gasses vary based on the water type.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] The Electrolyzer can now electrolyze all water types, resulting in different gas mixes produced
[*] Polluted Water creates hydrogen and polluted oxygen
[*] Salt Water and Brine produce hydrogen, oxygen and chlorine (in varying quantities, according to their mixture)
[*] Power Consumption, Output Temperature are configurable for every water type
[*] Integration with "piped everything", "piped outputs" to either disable pipes on the electrolyzer building or adding pipes for additional elements
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
