[h1]Biome Trims Fixed[/h1]

This mod readds biome specific trims

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
Biomes in this game each have a specific trim that (in theory) applies to their tiles, 
causing each biomes tiles to have a specific tile border.
This mechanic broke in the "Hot Shots" Update, leading to all biomes having the same tile border.
This mod fixes that.
