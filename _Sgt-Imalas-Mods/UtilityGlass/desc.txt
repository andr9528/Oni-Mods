[h1]Utility Glass[/h1]

This mod adds Window Walls and Reinforced Window Tiles.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.1.1[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Window Wall: made from 100kg transparent (glass or diamond). Works like drywall, but is transparent so you can see the stars behind it in space.
[*] Reinforced Window Tile: made from 100kg steel and 50kg transparent (glass or diamond). light and decor can pass through it, immune to meteor damage.
[/list]
