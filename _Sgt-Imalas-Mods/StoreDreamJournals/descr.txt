[h1]Store Dream Journals[/h1]

This tiny mod allows to store dream journals. This allows shipping them to other asteroids or store them for later without cluttering the base

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Dream Journals can now be stored inside any non-food-exclusive storage.
[*] Found under "Industrial Ingredients".
[/list]
