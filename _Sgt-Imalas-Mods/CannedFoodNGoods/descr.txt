[h1]Canned Food[/h1]

This mod adds a canned barbeque and canned tuna, non spoiling food variants that are made at the crafting station.

[h1]Current latest Version: 3.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] 
New canned Food variants: [list]
[*]Canned Barbeque (made from Barbeque)
[*]Canned Tuna (made from Cooked Seafood)
[*]Canned Bread (made from Pepper Bread)[/list]
[*] all foods are made at the crafting station.
[*] canning requires 500g of (refined) Copper / Aluminum / Steel (material is configurable in Mod Settings, default is Copper.) and 500g of the respective food, resulting in half calorie density of the non-canned material when canned
[*] does not spoil
[*] designed for space missions
[*] once eaten, can scraps are dropped, those can be recycled back into the metal used on construction
[/list]

[h1]DLC Features[/h1]
[list][*] Canned Tuna will provide Radiation Resistance, similar to normal sea food.
[/list]
