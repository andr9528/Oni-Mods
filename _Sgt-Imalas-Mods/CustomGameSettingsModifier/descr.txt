[h1]Modify Difficulty Settings[/h1]

This mod allows you to adjust the game difficulty settings of an existing game without needing to use a savegame editor.

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]
[hr][/hr]

[h1]Modifiable Settings[/h1][list]
[*] Disease Resistence
[*] Hunger
[*] Morale
[*] Suit Durability
[*] Meteor Shower Intensity
[*] Radiation Resistence (DLC only)
[*] Stress
[*] Stress Reactions
[*] Care Packages
[*] Fast Workers Mode
[/list]

[h1]Process:[/h1]
1. Go to the pause menu and click the new button ("Change Difficulty Settings") to open the modify
2. Make your modifications and close the window.
3. Save the game(!)
4. Load the save created in step 3(!).
5. Success - the settings are now active

(settings that do not require a reload: Care Packages & Fast Workers Mode)
