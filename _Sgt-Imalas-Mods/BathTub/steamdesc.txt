[h1]Bubbles Bathtub[/h1]

This mod adds a fancy bathtub to the game duplicants can use as an alternative to showering.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/GbFtLZ2.png[/img]

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] New Building: Bathtub
[*] Requires 500kg of precious rock (obsidian/granite) and 200kg of ore.
[*] Unlocked with the research "Sanitation".
[*] Up to 3 duplicants can fit in one bathtub.
[*] Bathing takes a longer than a quick shower, it's simply too cozy in there (35secs).
[*] During bathing duplicants will receive the hottub relax buff (-20% stress per cycle for the bathing duration).
[*] Comes with an extra handy retro tub skin.
[/list]
