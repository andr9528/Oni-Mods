[h1]Pedestal Filter[/h1]

This mod adds a text filter and an artifact filter to the pedestal screen

[h1]Current latest Version: 1.0.0[/h1]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list][*] Pedestal Text Filter: Filter items in the pedestal selection
[*] Pedestal Artifact Filter: toggle only showing artifacts
[*] Gas+Liquid Tints: liquid and gas icons are no longer white
[*] Mod Config: toggle the artifact filter to be enabled by default
[/list]
